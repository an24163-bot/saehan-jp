Saehan Nanotech Featured on Maeil Business TV as an 'Excellent Company'

Saehan Nanotech Co., Ltd. was featured on Maeil Business TV's "Excellent Company" segment, showcasing the company's capabilities in precision semiconductor machine tool manufacturing.

The program highlighted Saehan Nanotech's complete product lineup of 8 core machine types — including both-side drilling machines, multi-wire and single-wire sawing machines, grinding centers, core drilling machines, polishing machines, vertical grinding machines, and nano-imprinting machines.

The feature also covered the company's vertically integrated manufacturing model, where all machines are designed, manufactured, assembled, tested, and serviced at the company's Changwon headquarters and factory facility.

Saehan Nanotech serves customers across the semiconductor, display, EV, renewable energy, and advanced ceramics industries, with export experience to the United States, China, and other markets.
