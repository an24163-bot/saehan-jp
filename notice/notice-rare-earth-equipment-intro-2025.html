Introducing Saehan Nanotech's Rare Earth Permanent Magnet Processing Equipment

Saehan Nanotech is proud to introduce its rare earth permanent magnet processing equipment — a specialized diamond wire sawing machine designed for precision slicing of NdFeB and SmCo sintered magnets.

As global demand for electric vehicles and wind power generators accelerates, the need for precision-cut rare earth magnets has grown dramatically. Saehan Nanotech's multi-wire diamond wire sawing technology addresses this demand with a machine that delivers high-volume batch slicing while minimizing kerf loss and material waste.

Key Features

  • Diamond wire sawing with water-based coolant — safe processing of fire-risk metallic materials
  • Proprietary tilting and rotating mechanism — up to 3x productivity vs. conventional methods
  • Material-specific recipe control — optimized cutting parameters for NdFeB, SmCo, and ferrite magnets
  • Fully customized machine configuration — wire guide geometry, coolant systems, and tensioning per order

For inquiries about rare earth magnet processing equipment, please contact us at info@saehannanotech.com.
